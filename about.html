Meet Our Founder

Amit Gupta, a visionary leader with over two decades of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, founded The Vill with a singular mission: to revolutionize women's healthcare. After a distinguished career at leading global pharma companies, Amit was driven by the need for a more focused, patient-centric approach to gynecological and obstetric medicine. He envisioned a company that would not just sell products, but would build a legacy of trust and empowerment for women everywhere.

Amit's expertise in clinical research and his passion for innovation have been instrumental in shaping The Vill's product portfolio and its unwavering commitment to quality.
