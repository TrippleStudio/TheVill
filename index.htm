Frequently Asked Questions

What types of birth control do you provide?

We offer a full spectrum of hormonal contraceptives, including combined oral contraceptives (COCs), progestin-only pills (POPs), hormonal IUDs, patches, and injectables to suit diverse patient needs and preferences.

Are your medications FDA approved?

Absolutely. All our medications are FDA-approved and sourced from leading, reputable pharmaceutical manufacturers. We adhere to the highest standards of quality, safety, and efficacy for all our products.

Do you offer hormone replacement therapy (HRT)?

Yes, we provide comprehensive and individualized hormone replacement therapies for managing menopause symptoms, including tailored estrogen and progesterone treatments designed for optimal patient outcomes.
